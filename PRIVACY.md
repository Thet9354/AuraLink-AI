# AuraLink AI — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-05_

**AuraLink AI collects no data and has no network access.** Everything the app does happens on your
device.

## What the app accesses (and why)

- **Camera** — to recognize American Sign Language in real time. Frames are processed on device and
  are never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
- **Microphone** — to caption speech, detect important sounds, and derive haptic feedback. Audio is
  processed on device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
- **Speech recognition** — transcription runs **on device only** (`requiresOnDeviceRecognition`).
  The app refuses to transcribe if on-device recognition is unavailable rather than sending audio to
  a server.

## What is stored

- **Sign examples you record** (in Enroll) stay on your device, **encrypted at rest** with a
  device-only key. They are used solely to recognize your signing.
- **App settings** are stored in the standard on-device preferences.

## What is never done

- No account, no sign-in.
- No analytics, no tracking, no advertising identifiers.
- No network requests — the app links no networking APIs at all.
- No data shared with the developer or any third party.

Because there is no networking code in the app, data leaving your device is not merely disallowed by
policy — it is impossible by construction.
